Welcome! I am half a political scientist and half a data scientist. I am interested in conflict and stability, the Asia-Pacific, policy analysis, and Bayesian inference for decision making.

I am currently a Lecturer at the School of Law and Government, Dublin City University. I was an Irish Research Council Government of Ireland Postdoctoral Fellow from 2018 to 2020 at the School of Politics and International Relations, University College Dublin, with which I remain affiliated as an Adjunct Research Fellow. I was a Max Weber Fellow at the European University Institute from 2016 to 2018. I obtained my PhD from Dublin City University in 2015.

This website introduces some of my research. Obviously, the views expressed are my own unless otherwise stated, and do not necessarily represent those of the organizations/institutes to which I am/have been related.
